📚 SmartStudy-AI-Powered Academic Scheduler

Time management is one of the biggest challenges students face in academic environments. Balancing assignments, exams, projects, and personal commitments often becomes overwhelming, leading to missed deadlines and increased stress.

This project introduces an AI-powered academic scheduler designed to simplify and optimize student planning. By leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs), the system intelligently organizes and prioritizes weekly tasks based on user-provided inputs such as:

Task descriptions
Deadlines
Estimated completion time
Priority/importance level

Using these inputs, the scheduler generates a structured and optimized weekly plan that helps students:

📌 Manage their time more effectively
📈 Improve productivity
🧠 Reduce academic stress
🎯 Stay consistent with their goals

The system adapts dynamically to different workloads, making it a flexible and reliable study companion for students across various academic levels.
